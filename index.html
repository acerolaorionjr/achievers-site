About Achievers High School

Achievers High School was founded under the Achievers Group of Education & Training Organization. The group started as Achievers Continuing Education Centre in Ibadan, which provided professional exams tuition (CIBN, ATS, ICAN), GCE & JAMB coaching. From this foundation, Achievers High School in Ibadan was formally established, serving as a feeder / screening centre for Achievers University, Owo. 0

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide quality education that develops competent, morally grounded, and responsible students who excel academically and become leaders in society.

Vision: To be a center of excellence in secondary education, upholding the values of academic integrity, leadership, and service. (Values drawn from the Achievers University motto: Knowledge, Integrity & Leadership) 1

Affiliation

This school is part of the Achievers network of institutions. It is affiliated with Achievers University, Owo, Ondo State. Achievers University was licensed in 2007 and has its roots in the Achievers Continuing Education Centre in Ibadan. 2
