Our Heritage & Vision

The Achievers Educational Group began its journey of academic excellence under the visionary leadership of Hon. Dr. Bode Ayorinde. What launched in 1991 as a dedicated Continuous Education Centre in Ibadan quickly evolved into an expansive educational legacy.

Driven by the ultimate goal to provide qualitative, value-driven education across multiple frameworks, the group expanded into secondary education with the establishment of Achievers High School in Ibadan, and subsequently achieved premier tertiary standing with the birth of Achievers University, Owo in 2007.

Today, our institutions serve thousands of students, holding firm to our foundational tripartite creed: nurturing deep academic exploration, moral standing, and civic execution.

Frequently Asked Admissions Questions

1. How do I clear my credentials at the Ibadan Liaison Office?

Prospective candidates within the Western region can submit hard-copy credentials for vetting directly at the Arulogun Road screening office before final clearance at Owo.

2. What is the approved baseline UTME score for Achievers University?

The standard cutoff point for admission cycles is 150, though higher scores are required for professional tiers like Law and Nursing.
